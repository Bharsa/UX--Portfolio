Wash Ease project- IIIT Bangalore

As part of our HCI course project at IIIT Bangalore, we designed the student-centric laundry system, Wash Ease. My role in the team was a UX writer and user researcher. I conducted user interviews, performed usability testing, and documented the project progress to replace the existing physical card-based laundry system with a user-friendly design.

Technography- Mobile usage among senior citizens

In my Technography individual project, I conducted a user study on senior citizens' use of mobile technology to identify their pain points and suggest improvements. By conducting five interviews at an elderly care home, I gained valuable experience in user research, developed my skills in understanding user needs, and designing solutions.

Qulitative research project- OTT vs theatres

For my qualitative research project, I studied the movie-watching experience on OTT platforms vs. in theaters. I conducted literature review and interviews, I gained valuable insights into the decision-making process and impact on the experience, leading to a better understanding of the nuances of movie-watching.

DonateHub: Empowering IIITB's Future

As part of our group project to create an app that facilitates donations to IIIT Bangalore, my role was to conduct a soft systems methodology (SSM) to identify the key issues and challenges faced by students in affording higher education at IIIT Bangalore. To do this, I conducted interviews with the registrar, students, and placement committee members to gather their perspectives on the scholarships, fees and donation process. Using the insights gathered through the SSM and interviews, I played a key role in designing the alumni sponsorship page and login page for the app.
